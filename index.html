Google’s Official Policy Around Reviews

Google makes it clear that feedback should be voluntary and honest. Any attempt to manipulate or trade ratings is treated as a violation that can lead to penalties and long term damage to credibility.

Key Rule: Reviews must be based on genuine experiences without compensation or influence.
